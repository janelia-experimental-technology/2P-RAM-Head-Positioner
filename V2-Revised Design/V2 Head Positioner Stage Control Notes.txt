The Motorized stages in the V2 Head Positioner design are controlled by Zaber stepper motor controllers. These in combination with the Zaber Console software allow easy control and positioning of the three axes. The V2 positioner has one Zaber rotary stage and two stepper motor driven stages from Optics Focus ( the goniometer and Z stage). Adapter cables were made connecting the DB9 connectors on the Optics Focus stages to the Zaber Controllers. Pinout diagrams can be found on the Optics Focus and Zaber websites for reference. The original Zaber A-MCA controllers are now obsolete and we recommend the X-MCC 3 axis controller as a suitable replacement.
