The Motorized stages in the V1 Head Positioner design are controlled by Zaber stepper motor controllers. These in combination with the Zaber Console software allow easy control and positioning of the three axes. The V1 positioner has three Zaber stages which connect directly to the Zaber Controllers. The original Zaber A-MCA controllers are now obsolete and we recommend the X-MCC 3 axis controller as a suitable replacement.
