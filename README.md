### Head Positioner for 2P-RAM 2P-RAM Imaging

The motorized head positioner/goniometer allows the user to position the headbar in an automated, repeatable location underneath the microscope. It was designed to work with the 2P-RAM microscope objective and headbars, and leaves the sides of the mouse’s head unobstructed so that camera lenses or other equipment may be mounted close to the head. The device provides automated rotation of a head-fixed rodent specimen about the anterior-posterior and medio-lateral axes for imaging or other purposes. The rotation center of the device is the center of the headbar window opening. The device also provides automated Z translation and is designed to be compatible with behavioral setups such as the large spherical and other treadmills. There are two designs contained herein. The first is designed using stages from Zaber only and is included for reference. The second design which we recommend uses a rotation stages from Zaber and two other stages from Optics Focus which provide a more stable platform. 



Specifications:



&#x20;   Rotation range around Anterior-Posterior axis : +/-20 degrees

&#x20;   Rotation range around Medio-Lateral axis : +/- 8 degrees

&#x20;   Z travel: +/- 10 mm
